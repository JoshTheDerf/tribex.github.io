Uppercase Those Hex Strings!

Hexadecimal strings (you know, those six-character words with numbers and letters from a-f in them, often prefixed by a # character?) are a fundamental part of any designer or front-end developer's daily routine. They are one of the simplest and most widely supported ways to specify colors in CSS. It comes as a surprise then that those who use them have not yet settled on the letter case to use when writing such strings...
